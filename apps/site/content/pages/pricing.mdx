## Junk Removal Pricing Made Simple

Pricing is based on **trailer volume** (how full the load is) and access. We give firm, on-site estimates before any work begins and back every job with our 48-hour make-it-right guarantee.

### Trailer Volume Example Ranges

Most jobs fall between **$175–$900** depending on volume and labor/access. Use these example ranges as a starting point — final pricing is confirmed on-site.

| Trailer fill | Example range |
| --- | --- |
| ¼ load | $175–$250 |
| ½ load | $350–$500 |
| ¾ load | $525–$700 |
| Full load | $700–$900 |

### Common Add-ons

- Mattress: +$30 each
- Paint cans: +$10 each
- Tires: +$10 each

> Other surcharges may apply for freon, hazardous materials, and heavy/specialty items.

### What's Included

- Two-person crew, lifting and removal from where items sit
- Responsible disposal and recycling whenever possible
- Sweep-up and light tidy of the pickup area

### Commercial & Construction

- Offices, retail cleanouts, and light construction debris are quoted on-site based on volume, access, and material type.
- We accommodate 7 AM-7 PM scheduling and after-hours windows.
- COIs, vendor onboarding, and multi-site quotes available on request.

### What Can Change the Price

- Distance to the items (curbside vs inside pickup, stairs, elevators, long carries)
- Material type (mixed junk vs dense debris like dirt/brick/roofing)
- Heavy/special items (pianos, safes, large hot tubs, etc.)
- Surcharges for hazardous materials, freon appliances, or restricted disposal items

> Need help choosing? Use the "Get instant quote" button or contact us. Final pricing depends on load size, access, and material type.
