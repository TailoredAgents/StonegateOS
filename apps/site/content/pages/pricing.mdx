## Junk Removal Pricing Made Simple

Pricing is based on load size (volume) and access. We give firm, on‑site estimates before any work begins and back every job with our 48‑hour make‑it‑right guarantee.

### Residential Ranges

- Single items (chair, small appliance): typically $75–$125
- Quarter load (3–4 cubic yards): typically $150–$250
- Half load (6–8 cubic yards): typically $280–$420
- Full load (12–15 cubic yards): typically $480–$780
- Surcharges may apply for special items (paint, tires, freon).

### What's Included

- Two‑person crew, lifting and removal from where items sit
- Responsible disposal and recycling whenever possible
- Sweep‑up and light tidy of the pickup area

### Commercial & Construction

- Offices, retail cleanouts, and light construction debris are quoted on‑site based on volume, access, and material type.
- We accommodate 7 AM–7 PM scheduling and after‑hours windows.
- COIs, vendor onboarding, and multi‑site quotes available on request.
